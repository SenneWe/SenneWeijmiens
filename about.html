Hello there!

I'm Senne, a 20-year-old IT enthusiast currently in my final year of Electronics-ICT with a focus on systems security and services at Thomasmore Campus de Nayer. My journey into the world of technology began in secondary school, where I delved into Electromechanics. This diverse field exposed me to a plethora of subjects, ranging from electricity, physics, and mechanics to hydraulic and pneumatic systems, PLC concepts, 3D drawing, and even some CNC.

Every summer since the age of 15, I've engaged in various roles through student jobs at La William, Belgian Post, and Caterpillar. These experiences have given me a well-rounded perspective on different facets of the professional world.

Beyond the realm of IT, I find joy in diverse interests. Riding my motorcycle brings me a sense of freedom and exhilaration. Sports shooting has become a fulfilling hobby, allowing me to sharpen my focus and precision. I explore my creative side through amateur photography, capturing moments that inspire me. On a more unique note, I am also a watch enthusiast, and I take pride in building my own timepieces under the brand Ludovica.

When I'm not immersed in the world of technology and craftsmanship, you can find me enjoying some downtime gaming. This balance between my technical pursuits and personal interests defines who I am—a dynamic individual passionate about exploring the intersections of technology, creativity, and life experiences.

Welcome to my portfolio, where I showcase the fusion of my academic knowledge, professional experiences, and personal hobbies. I invite you to explore the diverse facets of my journey and the projects that reflect my enthusiasm for all things electronic and beyond.
