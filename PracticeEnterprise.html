Bluesky Unlimited

Welcome to the world of Practice Enterprise! In this exciting project, we embarked on a thrilling journey to simulate the startup of a company from scratch. Our mission was to build an entire ecosystem, where we established the foundation of the enterprise.

Bluesky Unlimited

So what is Bluesky Unlimited? It is a small made-up company that negotiates, buys and sells cargo space on airplanes. It then provides a centralised platform where you can find available cargospace from many diffrent airlines, all in one place. Customers can then very easly buy cargo space for their shipments on this platform. For this company to function, there where several things that needed to be set-up: ICT infrastructure, Software, AI tools and much more.

All students from the second phase Electronics-ICT participated to this project, and together we brought the Practice Enterprise to life. It served as a testbed for our skills, enabling us to refine our abilities in the realm of IT. Throughout this venture, teamwork and creativity were the driving forces behind our success.

The Teams

Security, Systems & Services
Application Development
Applied Artificial Intelligence

The "Security, Systems & Services" team (my team) diligently laid the groundwork, creating a robust IT infrastructure that included Windows servers, NFS, security cameras, a reliable network infrastructure, webservers and much more. Security was at the forefront of our efforts, ensuring the company's sensitive data and assets were safeguarded against any potential threats.

The "Application Development" team joined the endeavor, crafting innovative applications that formed the face of our enterprise. From designing an intuitive website that showcased our products and services to developing software solutions tailored to our unique needs.

The "Artificial Intelligence" team. By leveraging the power of AI, they created sophisticated models capable of predicting growth, analyzing trends, and optimizing various aspects of the enterprise. Their work would allow the enterprise to make data-driven decisions and stay ahead of the competition.
